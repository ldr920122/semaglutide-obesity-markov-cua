## Gate Finding

The original version was transparent but at risk of appearing methodologically thin compared with recent multi-complication obesity economic models. The revised version is more defensible because it adds:

1. a benefit-price frontier that quantifies how much omitted non-diabetes benefit would be needed to change the conclusion;
2. PSA-based driver correlations;
3. per-patient EVPI outputs;
4. an explicit method-precedent matrix.

The manuscript should still avoid claiming to be a comprehensive HTA, a systematic review, or an independent external validation of CADTH.
